CROWDFUNDING 2023 - 2024
Team Eta is a student team of KJSCE which manufactures prototypes and is aiming for urban-class vehicles for the Shell Eco-Marathon. Team Eta is among those brilliant teams that have successfully manufactured super mileage vehicles that can reach a mileage of 268.7 kmpL. The team has been actively participating in this event for the past decade. The team is ready to compete on a larger scale it needs your help.
Team Eta will now be competing in the Shell Eco-Marathon Asia which will be held in Indonesia this June. With this comes the expense of logistics, Manufacturing a vehicle that will be worthy enough to represent India on the Asia level, procurement of good quality raw materials, etc. We hope that the team gets your support for fulfilling the aim of zero carbon emission which is the real spirit of this event!
